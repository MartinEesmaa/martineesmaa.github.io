I made a freakshake in my school. :)

So here is how to make your own freakshake like a milkshake...

Before we get started, you'll need some equipment:

  • 1 jug
  • Chocolate balls to melt in the microwave (for serving)
  • 1 blender
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Milk
  • Any syrup (e.g., chocolate)
  • Any sweet and/or sour foods
  • Medium plate
Step Description
1 Choose any a syrup and put it into jug and blender machine.
2 Start by scooping a generous amount of vanilla ice cream into the blender.
3 Pour in a suitable amount of milk, depending on your desired thickness.
4 Blend the mixture until it's smooth and creamy.
5 When the shake is ready, pour the blended shake into a large glass or jug.
6 Now, it's time to get creative. Top your freakshake with sweet and/or sour foods like cookies, candy, whipped cream, or anything you like!
7 For an extra touch, melt some chocolate balls in the microwave and drizzle the melted chocolate over the top.
8 Place your freakshake on a medium plate to catch any delicious drips.
9 Enjoy your homemade freakshake!
